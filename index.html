I started my journey at University of Kansas majoring in Secondary English Education. After graduation in May 2009, I immediately went into my Masters Degree program. After completing my Masters of Education in Curriculum and Instruction, I worked for ten years in public education. As a teacher with ten years of experience working in public education, I learned to quickly adapt to changes, solve problems on the fly, and work well with my team. In my last years in the classroom I began teaching students, especially girls, to code. The more I worked with them and their new skills, the more I wanted to get into development myself. When the military moved my family to Guam, it was the perfect opportunity to make a career shift. I completed a six month Full Stack Development bootcamp and have been working on developing my skills and portfolio since graduation.
